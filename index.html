About Sunday Crumb

At Sunday Crumb Sourdough Co., we specialize in small-batch artisan sourdough baked with patience and care. Every loaf and bagel is naturally fermented and handcrafted to develop rich flavor, a satisfying texture, and the distinctive character that makes sourdough so special.

Our breads are perfect for gathering around the table—whether you're hosting family and friends, creating a beautiful charcuterie board, serving brunch, celebrating a holiday, or simply elevating an everyday meal.
